About Me

From complexity of space science to simpler molecular reactions of chemistry to language and programming of computers, I have been captivated by the wonders of science and technology. My interest for science goes back to the time I was at school. Having represented my school in Science and cyber Olympiad for six consecutive years, I have been exposed to variety of complex problems which demands high level of analytical ability. Participation in intensive training programs and earning recognition of merit at early age has not only boosted by confidence but also sown the seeds of my future endeavors. When all around my friends were busy enjoying video games I was more interested in exploring Computer on internet. My personal interest lies in computer programming and software developing as it requires problem solving and application of logical thinking. To expand my basic knowledge into a professional qualification, I want to pursue my education in the computer field. Counting at the future prospects I wish to gain vast and in depth knowledge of computer field which not only be fertile for me but to my country’s growth as well.
